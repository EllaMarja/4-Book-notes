Ella's Book Reviews


*this was never meant to be published ...


Starting on february 2023, I wanted to explore the world of books. I had never been a fan of reading, I would argue school hadn't presented it in the best of ways.
During this moment, I found myself with a lot of time on my hands. I discovered I loved to learn new things, something I now had to manage and motivate myself to do. This lead me into dipping my toes into the 'self-help' waters. A handful of fantastic books to kick start my journey, dragged me into this amazing world of discovery, questions and knowledge. Bubbling in excited to share all that I learnt, I decided to write a personal 'review' to avoid forgetting my thoughts about each book. Enjoy!
